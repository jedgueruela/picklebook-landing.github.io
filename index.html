How does PICkle Book work?

PICkle Book is a subscription-based service that lets you upload your favorite photos every month and turns them into an organized printed book of photos that is shipped to your house. Just upload, review, and submit — we'll take care of the rest!

How many photos can I upload per month?

Each book has 50 of your cherished images preserved in an organized book. If you need additional PICkle Books beyond your monthly subscription, you can purchase add on credits.

In order to maintain an amazing low price on PICkle Book, we optimize manufacturing by keeping the book consistent in every way possible. Your PICkle Book will always contain 50 images. If you only upload 30 images in a given monthly anniversary period we will randomly duplicate images to achieve 50 in your book.

When will I be charged?

You will be charged at the start of your subscription membership and then again on your subsequent monthly assigned anniversary day. The anniversary day will match your start day unless you start your subscription on the 26th, 27th, 28th, 29th, 30th, or 31st of a month. In this case your account will have the anniversary day assigned as the 25th of each subsequent month.

We follow this reassignment of the anniversary day due to variability of days in a given month and to optimize our manufacturing process by proactively addressing traditionally heavy volume spikes during the first week of each month.

Can I update my shipping address?

You can update your shipping address at any time under your account settings. Please note that once an order has been submitted to manufacturing, the current address on file has been locked with the order and is not able to be changed.

How can I cancel my subscription?

You can easily cancel your subscription in your account settings. Note that credits purchased will not be refunded. If You cancel your subscription, you will have 90 days from the cancellation date to use the credits in your account. After 90 days, your previously purchased PICkle Book Credits and unused PICkle Book Credits will expire.

If you choose to delete your Account, any outstanding credits in your account at the time of deletion will expire immediately.

Is my payment information safe?

Yes! We utilize a major third-party platform payment service that is widely used by millions of businesses around the world. They hold the highest level of certifications, encryption, and compliance.
